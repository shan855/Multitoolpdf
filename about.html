Our Story

Founded in 2023, MultiTool was born from a simple idea: document processing should be secure, private, and accessible to everyone. We noticed that most online tools required uploading sensitive documents to servers, compromising user privacy.

Our team of developers set out to create a different kind of toolset - one where all processing happens 100% in your browser. No file uploads, no server storage, just pure client-side processing that respects your privacy.

Our Mission

We believe that everyone deserves access to powerful document processing tools without compromising their privacy. Our mission is to provide:

  • Privacy-First Tools: All processing happens locally in your browser
  • No Limitations: Free access with no watermarks or restrictions
  • Cross-Platform: Works on all devices and browsers
  • Continuous Improvement: Regular updates and new features
