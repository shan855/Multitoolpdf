Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. We never upload or store your files.

Last Updated: February 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to MultiTool. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our services. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and tools.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any of your files or personal data. All PDF and image processing happens entirely in your browser (client-side). Your files never leave your device. This is the core principle of MultiTool.

Key Privacy Features

  • ✅ 100% Client-Side: All processing happens in your browser
  • ✅ No Uploads: Your files never leave your device
  • ✅ No Storage: We don't store any files on servers
  • ✅ No Tracking: We don't track your file usage
  • ✅ No Logs: We don't keep any logs of your activity

3. Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage only for:

  • Theme Preference: Save your light/dark mode preference
  • Newsletter Subscribers: If you subscribe, your email is stored locally for demo purposes (never sent to any server)

This data never leaves your browser and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Fonts: For typography. May collect IP address.
  • Font Awesome: For icons. May collect usage data.
  • PDF Libraries: Open-source libraries (pdf-lib, PDF.js) that run locally in your browser.

These services are used solely for functionality and do not have access to your files.

5. Cookies

MultiTool does not use tracking cookies. We only use local storage for theme preferences, which is not a cookie and stays on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@multitool.com
  • Website: Contact Page

Your privacy is not just a policy - it's our promise.
